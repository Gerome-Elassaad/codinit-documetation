# Teams
**Collaborate on your app in real time with teammates**

CodinIT lets you build apps together, live. Invite your designer, developer, agency, or anyone else to your workspace. Everyone sees changes as they happen.

Each subscription is now connected to a workspace:

- **Users with a Pro subscription** have personal workspaces. You can still invite up to 2 people to individual projects. Collaborators on a project use project owner credits.
- **Users with a Teams subscription** can have up to 20 users in their workspace. Owners & admins can freely invite & remove users and manage projects. Editors can only make edits to existing projects. All users in a workspace share the connected credit pool.

## Real-Time Collaboration

Once teammates join your workspace, you'll experience seamless real-time collaboration:

- **Live cursors**: You'll see their cursor and live changes as they work
- **Team presence indicators**: Their icons appear in the top-right corner showing who's online
- **Instant editing**: You can edit any element together, instantly
- **Synchronized changes**: All modifications are reflected immediately across all team members

### Role Descriptions

- **Owner**: Full administrative control over the workspace, billing, and all projects
- **Admin**: Can manage most aspects of the workspace and projects, but cannot modify other admin roles
- **Editor**: Can edit and publish projects but cannot manage workspace settings, integrations, or user permissions
